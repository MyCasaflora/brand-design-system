05 — Visual

Imagery

Photography and illustration guidelines that reinforce our brand character and emotional resonance.

Composition Example

Natural Lighting

Prefer soft, directional light. Avoid harsh flash or artificial overhead lighting.

Subject Example

Authentic Subjects

Real people in genuine moments. No staged or overly polished stock photography.

Color Grading Example

Warm Color Grade

Subtle warm toning aligned with our palette. Maintain natural skin tones.

Perspective Example

Dynamic Angles

Slightly off-center compositions create energy. Avoid perfectly centered, static frames.

Context Example

Environmental Context

Show products and people within their real environments, not on plain backdrops.

Texture Example

Texture & Detail

Close-up detail shots emphasize craft and quality. Celebrate materiality.

We design for everyone. These standards ensure our brand is perceivable, operable, and understandable by all users.

Touch Targets

  • Minimum 44 × 44px for all interactive elements
  • 8px minimum spacing between adjacent targets
  • Visible hover and active states on all controls
  • Adequate padding on links within body text

Focus Management

  • 2px solid primary-500 outline on :focus-visible
  • 2px outline-offset for comfortable spacing
  • Logical tab order follows visual hierarchy
  • Skip-to-content link on all pages

Screen Readers

  • Semantic HTML elements for all content sections
  • Descriptive alt text on all meaningful images
  • ARIA labels on icon-only interactive elements
  • Visually hidden text for context where needed
  • Live regions for dynamic content updates
